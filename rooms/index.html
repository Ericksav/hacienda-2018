Our pet policy

pet friendly hotels

General rules

  • All quiet and non-aggressive pets are welcomed.
  • All pets must be vaccinated, clean, and free of fleas and ticks.
  • Weight must not exceed 20 lbs. per pet.
  • All pets must have a collar ID and remain on a leash.

Room rules

  • All pets must sleep under the owner’s care.
  • Pets are not allowed to sleep on any of the room’s beds or sofas.
  • Pets cannot be showered inside room’s bathroom.

Patio rules

  • Pets must be supervised by their owners at all times while spending time at the patio.
  • Pets must wear a collar and be on a leash at all times.
  • TOwners are responsible for cleaning up after their pet.

Guests will be responsible for the expenses incurred by the hotel to restore the property to the condition it was before the damages. This includes, but is not limited to, any professional cleaning, furniture or electronic devices that need replacement. As well as loses incurred by the hotel for not renting the room while it's out of service being repaired or cleaned.
