Lodging Promotion for Dental Tourists

If you are a tourist looking for dental work in Los Algodones you don’t have to look for any Yuma Hotels. Now you can enjoy your stay at Hotel Hacienda Los Algodones for free while having dental treatment with premium dentists in Los Algodones.

This promotion applies only with the participant dental clinics: Sani Dental Group and Sani Platinum.

All Sani Dental Group patients are eligible to receive $15 off their dental treatment for each night they book at Hacienda Los Algodones Hotel.

ONE FREE NIGHT AT HOTEL HACIENDA LOS ALGODONES

You can take advantage of this promotion and enjoy 1 free night at Hotel Hacienda Los Algodones if your dental treatment is over $1, 000 USD.

Restrictions:

  • It does not apply with other promotions or discounts
  • This promotion is individual and non-transferable
  • Apply on a Single room (2 party) only
  • Subject to hotel availability

THREE FREE NIGHTS AT HOTEL HACIENDA LOS ALGODONES

You can take advantage of this promotion and enjoy up to 3 free nights at Hotel Hacienda Los Algodones if your dental treatment is over $2, 000 USD.

Restrictions:

  • It does not apply with other promotions or discounts
  • This promotion is individual and non-transferable
  • Apply on a Single or Double room (up to 4 party)
  • Subject to hotel availability

Hotel Bill Rebate

Guests are eligible to receive part or the total amount of their hotel bill deducted from the total value of their dental treatment from Sani Dental Group.
