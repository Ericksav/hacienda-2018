Welcome to Los Algodones

Over the years, Hacienda Los Algodones has become medical tourists’ favorite hotel in Los Algodones. Our outstanding customer service, charming accommodations and enchanting atmosphere are just some of the reasons why our guests keep coming back.

We’re located just seven miles west of Yuma, AZ and 4 blocks away from the border crossing.

Stay with us, and we promise you’ll feel right at home.
