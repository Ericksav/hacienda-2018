Welcome to Los Algodones

Over the years, Hacienda Los Algodones in Los Algodones, Baja California has become the favorite hotel visited by medical tourists from around the world. Our outstanding customer service, charming accommodations and enchanting atmosphere are just some of the reasons why our guests keep coming back. We’re just seven miles west of Yuma, Az and 4 blocks away from the border crossing. Los Algodones is a friendly town filled with medical tourists, where everything is at walking distance. Local transportation is not necessary and if you wish, you can return to the United States by walking the border.

Stay with us, and we promise you’ll feel right at home.
